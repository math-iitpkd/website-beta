About the Department

The Mathematics Department at IIT Palakkad fosters excellence in research and teaching, with strengths across pure and applied areas. It offers PhD (since 2017) and MSc (since 2020) programs, and contributes to B.Tech and interdisciplinary M.Tech programs. Faculty expertise spans Algebra, Geometry, Analysis, Topology, Probability, and more. PhD students are actively involved as teaching assistants in undergraduate and postgraduate courses, gaining valuable academic experience. The department also runs the MATH CIRCLES outreach program to nurture school-level mathematical talent.
