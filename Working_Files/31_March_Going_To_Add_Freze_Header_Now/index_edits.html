About the Department

The Department of Mathematics at IIT Palakkad is a vibrant hub for research and teaching in pure and applied mathematics. With strengths spanning areas like Algebraic Geometry, Fluid Dynamics, Probability, and more, the department offers PhD (since 2017) and MSc (since 2020) programs with flexible, research-driven curricula. It actively contributes to interdisciplinary M.Tech programs and B.Tech courses, while nurturing talent through initiatives like Math Circles for school students. A growing, diverse faculty and committed research culture position the department for dynamic expansion.
