    Summer Training Programme in Mathematics

    Department of Mathematics

    Indian Institute of Technology Palakkad

    30 June - 10 July 2025

    Click to edit text

    Delete section
    Section colors
    Duplicate section
    Delete section

    The primary aim of this program is to identify young minds who are at the entry-level of mathematics, motivate and groom them to be competent enough to solve some of the challenging problems in mathematics. Towards achieving this goal, the workshop will give focused and concentrated training by conducting interactive sessions in core mathematics wherein the participants will be prompted to learn mathematics by doing it themselves. The programme is going to be conducted by the Faculty of the Department of Mathematics and sponsored by IIT Palakkad.
