Departmental Grievance Cell

Purpose

The departmental grievance committee looks into the grievances of students of the department and of research scholars associated with the department of Mathematics. The students/scholars may send an email to the chairperson of the committee or fill a form created for the same with their grievance. The cell has an official mail address, namely: dgrc_ma@iitpkd.ac.in. The grievance must be mailed to the above given mail address or better, the Google form below filled up. And, just to ensure that the grievance is noticed without any delay i.e., for speedy redressal, it is requested to put a cc of the grievance mail, to the email address of the current Chairperson as well. Students with grievances will then be contacted by the chair as soon as possible.

In case the grievance is on one of the members of this Grievance Committee, the student may approach either the Head of the Department, Mathematics or directly, the institute grievance cell.
